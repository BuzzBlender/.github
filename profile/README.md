# Buzzblender

Buzzblender is a cloud-based [dynamic digital signage platform](https://buzzblender.com/dynamic-digital-signage-software) built for businesses that are tired of overpriced or overly complex signage solutions.

The goal is simple: make digital signage easy, affordable, and actually useful in day-to-day business operations.

## Why Buzzblender?

Most signage platforms require expensive hardware, complicated setup, or long onboarding processes.

Buzzblender focuses on speed and simplicity:

- Works on any Smart TV ([LG](https://buzzblender.com/lg-webos-signage-player), [Samsung](https://buzzblender.com/samsung-signage-software), [Android](https://buzzblender.com/industries/android-tv-hotel-mode), [Philips](https://buzzblender.com/operating-systems/buzzblender-digital-signage-app-for-titan-os), and more)
- No additional hardware required
- Fully cloud-based remote management
- Setup takes just a few minutes
- [Pricing](https://buzzblender.com/pricing) starts at $5 per screen/month

## Key Features

### Unified Scheduling + Playlist Creation

Buzzblender is the first CMS that merges scheduling and playlist creation into a single workflow.

As a result, users can connect a device and schedule a playlist in as little as 4 minutes.

### QR Code Campaigns

Generate [QR codes](https://buzzblender.com/features/qr-code-for-digital-signage) directly for media, promotions, menus, lead capture, and customer engagement.

The admin panel also includes QR visit statistics, allowing businesses to measure campaign performance in real time.

### Simple Hotel Mode

BuzzBlender is the first digital signage solution to offer a [Simple Hotel Mode](https://buzzblender.com/industries/android-tv-hotel-mode) without requiring complex hospitality system integrations.

It also functions as a full-featured launcher with:

- App display
- Status bar support
- System settings access

This allows hotels and hospitality businesses to transform standard Smart TVs into branded guest experiences without expensive infrastructure.

## Results

Across multiple customers, businesses using rotating promotions and QR campaigns have reported around a **30% uplift in impulse purchases**.
